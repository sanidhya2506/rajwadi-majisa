Majisa Maa

Majisa is worshipped as a mother goddess in Rajasthan. The famous temple of Majisa is located in Jasol, Barmer district. Devotees from across India come here to seek blessings and peace.

Who is Majisa?

Majisa is believed to be the incarnation of the divine mother, protecting her devotees from evil and granting peace and prosperity.

Jasol Temple

The temple in Jasol is a center of devotion and cultural heritage, attracting devotees from across India throughout the year.

Festivals & Fairs

Every year, grand fairs and cultural programs are held in honor of Majisa, where thousands gather in celebration and faith.
